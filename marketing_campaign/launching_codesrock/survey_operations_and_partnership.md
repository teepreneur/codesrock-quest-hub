## Part 2: Teacher Empowerment & Classroom Mentorship

### 2. Teacher Empowerment Workshop (Week 2)
*Rate the effectiveness of the 6-hour intensive training workshop provided to your teaching staff:*
* **Content Pacing & Clarity:**
  - [ ] 5 – Punctual, well-paced, and highly practical (the mock classroom exercises were excellent)
  - [ ] 4 – Good balance of theory and practice
  - [ ] 3 – Neutral / Pacing was slightly too fast
  - [ ] 2 – Overwhelming / Pacing was too fast, not enough hands-on time
  - [ ] 1 – Unprepared trainers / Waste of school time
* **Confidence Level Post-Training:**
  - [ ] 5 – Teachers left fully confident to lead lessons independently
  - [ ] 4 – Teachers felt ready with minor support
  - [ ] 3 – Neutral / Teachers needed major supervision
  - [ ] 2 – Teachers felt anxious about leading sessions
  - [ ] 1 – Teachers did not understand how to use the kits
